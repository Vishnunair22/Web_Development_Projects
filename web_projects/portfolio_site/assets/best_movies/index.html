Best Movies of all time according to me

My Top 3 Picks

Top Gun Maverick

This is an accelerating film with incredible visuals and sounds. The movie follows the events leading up to a drone attack mission carried by the Top Gun team. The movie has genuine emotions and hooks you to the very end.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows- Part 2

This conclusion to J.K Rowling's fictional tale in the movies was exceptional.If you are a Harry Potter fan I can not recommend this film enough.

Free Guy

This is a fun filled film with a different take on the RPG world. The movie is very enjoyable and engaging to the very end.
